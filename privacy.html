Privacy Policy

Effective May 15, 2026

The short version: Imagine: Kids Coloring Book is designed for children. We do not collect personal information from children, we do not show ads, we do not sell data, and we do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Drawings, ready-made pages, free-use counters, and purchased page balances are saved on your device. When you ask the app to make a new AI coloring page, the prompt text and an app-scoped rate-limit token are sent through our image-generation proxy so the page can be created.

1. Who we are

Imagine: Kids Coloring Book ("the app", "we", "us") is developed and published by Garrett Woodside. You can contact us at garrettmichael07@icloud.com.

2. Designed for children (COPPA & Apple Kids Category)

Imagine: Kids Coloring Book is directed to children under 13 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA"), Apple's Kids Category guidelines, and applicable children's privacy laws (including GDPR-K where it applies).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require an account, sign-up, email, name, or location.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not include social features, chat, or user-to-user content.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs.
  • External links (such as this policy) are gated behind a parental challenge.

3. What information the app handles

On-device only

  • Drawings and coloring pages you create are saved locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share or export them using iOS's share sheet (which requires a parent).
  • Ready-made prompt pages and themed pack pages are bundled with the app and open locally when selected.
  • App preferences (such as whether onboarding has been shown, drawing settings, the free-generation counter, and themed-pack free-page choices) are stored locally.
  • Purchased AI page balance is stored locally on the device using Apple's Keychain. This lets the app know how many purchased custom pages remain.

Sent through our proxy to generate images

When a child types or speaks a prompt (e.g. "a dinosaur eating pizza"), that text is sent over HTTPS to our Cloudflare Worker proxy and then to OpenAI's image generation API so the service can return a black-and-white coloring page. We also send a random, app-scoped rate-limit token so the proxy can reduce abuse. We do not send a name, email address, Apple ID, precise location, contact list, or account profile with the prompt.

Like all internet services, our proxy provider and OpenAI may process standard technical request information, such as IP address and device/network metadata, as needed to deliver, secure, and operate the service. We do not use this information for advertising or tracking.

OpenAI's handling of API inputs is governed by their own privacy policy and API data-usage terms. As of the effective date above, OpenAI states that content submitted via the API is not used to train their models by default. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Voice input (optional)

If a child uses the microphone button to speak a prompt, Apple's Speech Recognition framework converts speech to text. The app requests microphone and speech recognition permission through iOS the first time this feature is used; these permissions can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings. When supported by the device, the app requests on-device recognition. We do not store audio.

Purchases and payments

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information, Apple ID, card details, or name. The app uses Apple's StoreKit framework to confirm purchases and unlock the item purchased.

  • Custom AI page packs are consumable purchases of 20, 50, or 100 custom coloring book pages. The remaining page balance is stored on the device.
  • Themed packs are ready-made coloring packs. Each pack lets you choose one page free; unlocking a pack is a non-consumable purchase that makes all pages in that pack available.
  • Legacy subscriptions, if previously purchased, may still be checked by StoreKit so prior access can be honored.

See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Crash and diagnostic data (optional, controlled by Apple)

If you have enabled "Share With App Developers" in iOS Settings, Apple may share anonymous crash reports with us to help fix bugs. This setting is controlled by you in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

4. Safety & guardrails

Because this app is designed for children and uses AI image generation, we've built multiple layers of safety into the experience:

On-device prompt filter

Before any prompt is sent through our proxy to OpenAI, it is checked on your device against a content-safety filter. Prompts that contain or attempt to elicit any of the following are blocked immediately, with a kid-friendly message asking for a different idea:

  • Sexual or nude content
  • Violence or graphic harm
  • Weapons
  • Drugs or controlled substances
  • Hate speech or slurs
  • Self-harm
  • Prompt-injection attempts or jailbreak phrases
  • Gibberish or non-language input

Coloring-book style enforcement

Every prompt is automatically wrapped with instructions that constrain the output to a black-and-white, kid-appropriate, whimsical coloring-book illustration. The child's wording alone cannot override this style.

OpenAI's own moderation

OpenAI's image-generation API applies its own safety classifiers on top of our filter. If OpenAI's system considers a request unsafe, the image is refused and the child is shown a gentle "let's try something else" message.

Parental gate on sensitive actions

A simple arithmetic challenge (designed to be solvable by adults but not by young children) protects:

  • Access to purchase screens
  • Opening any external link (such as this policy or Terms of Use)
  • Sharing a drawing to another app

No chat, no community, no uploads

The app has no messaging, no comments, no social feed, no public gallery, and no user accounts. A child cannot contact strangers through the app, and other users cannot contact a child through the app.

No ads, no tracking, no third-party SDKs

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or behavioral-tracking libraries. There is no IDFA use and no cross-app tracking.

Minimal data sent off-device

The only app content transmitted off-device during normal image generation is the filtered text prompt and an app-scoped rate-limit token sent through our proxy for image generation. Ready-made prompt cards, themed pack pages, drawings, and saved colorings stay on the device unless you choose to share or export them.

Reporting unsafe output

Our layered filters block the overwhelming majority of inappropriate content, but no AI system is perfect. If a coloring page ever looks inappropriate for a child, please email garrettmichael07@icloud.com with a description and we will investigate and update our filters.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).
  • We do not track users across apps or websites.
  • We do not use Facebook, Google Analytics, or any third-party ad/analytics SDKs.
  • We do not build user profiles.

6. Parental controls

  • External links and purchase screens are protected by a parental gate (a simple math challenge).
  • You can restrict in-app purchases on your child's device using iOS Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions.
  • You can delete all of the app's data at any time by deleting the app from the device.

7. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store personal information, there is typically nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. If you believe we have inadvertently received information about you or your child, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Data retention and security

Content stored on your device persists until you delete it or uninstall the app. Prompts sent through our proxy to OpenAI are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. If image generation fails, the app does not spend a free generation or purchased page for that failed request.

9. International users

The app is operated from the United States. Our proxy provider and OpenAI may process image-generation requests on infrastructure located in the United States and other countries where they operate. By using the app, you consent to this processing.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice through the app or App Store. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
