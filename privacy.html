Privacy Policy

Effective April 21, 2026

The short version: Imagine: Kids Coloring Book is designed for children. We do not collect personal information from children, we do not show ads, we do not sell data, and we do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Drawings are saved on your device. The only data that leaves your device is the text prompt you type, which is sent to OpenAI to generate a coloring page.

1. Who we are

Imagine: Kids Coloring Book ("the app", "we", "us") is developed and published by Garrett Woodside. You can contact us at support@example.com.

2. Designed for children (COPPA & Apple Kids Category)

Imagine: Kids Coloring Book is directed to children under 13 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA"), Apple's Kids Category guidelines, and applicable children's privacy laws (including GDPR-K where it applies).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require an account, sign-up, email, name, or location.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not include social features, chat, or user-to-user content.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs.
  • External links (such as this policy) are gated behind a parental challenge.

3. What information the app handles

On-device only

  • Drawings and coloring pages you create are saved locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share or export them using iOS's share sheet (which requires a parent).
  • App preferences (such as whether onboarding has been shown) are stored locally.

Sent to OpenAI to generate images

When a child types or speaks a prompt (e.g. "a dinosaur eating pizza"), that text is sent to OpenAI's image generation API ("DALL·E") so the service can return a black-and-white coloring page. OpenAI processes this text to generate the image and returns it to the app. We do not send any personal information with the prompt — no name, email, device ID, account identifier, or location.

OpenAI's handling of API inputs is governed by their own privacy policy and API data-usage terms. As of the effective date above, OpenAI states that content submitted via the API is not used to train their models by default. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Voice input (optional)

If a child uses the microphone button to speak a prompt, iOS's on-device speech recognition converts speech to text. The microphone permission is requested by iOS the first time this feature is used; it can be revoked anytime in Settings → Privacy → Microphone. We do not store audio.

Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information, Apple ID, or name. Apple provides us only with an anonymized subscription status (active or not). See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Crash and diagnostic data (optional, controlled by Apple)

If you have enabled "Share With App Developers" in iOS Settings, Apple may share anonymous crash reports with us to help fix bugs. This setting is controlled by you in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).
  • We do not track users across apps or websites.
  • We do not use Facebook, Google Analytics, or any third-party ad/analytics SDKs.
  • We do not build user profiles.

5. Parental controls

  • External links and the subscription screen are protected by a parental gate (a simple math challenge).
  • You can restrict in-app purchases on your child's device using iOS Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions.
  • You can delete all of the app's data at any time by deleting the app from the device.

6. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store personal information, there is typically nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. If you believe we have inadvertently received information about you or your child, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data retention and security

Content stored on your device persists until you delete it or uninstall the app. Prompts sent to OpenAI are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS.

8. International users

The app is operated from the United States. OpenAI's image-generation service processes requests on infrastructure located in the United States. By using the app, you consent to this processing.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice through the app or App Store. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact us

Questions or concerns from parents are always welcome. Email support@example.com.
